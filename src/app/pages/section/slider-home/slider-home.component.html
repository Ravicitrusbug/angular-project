Red Rocks

Why is Mars red?

Explore Karijini Gorge in Australia and Oak Creek Canyon in Sedona, AZ searching for clues to the environments that formed the red rocks there. Observe these rock formations at a variety of scales to gather these clues. Finally, apply understanding of red rock formation on Earth to red rock formation on Mars.
